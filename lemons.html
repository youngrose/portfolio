LEMONS

Lemons is a 16 page, two-folio art book of postcards that was letterpress
printed and hand-bound during a concentration at Penland School of Craft in 2018.
Each page contains prints with perforated tear-lines and postcard markings
on the other side, to be popped out and mailed at your leisure. Edition of 18.
